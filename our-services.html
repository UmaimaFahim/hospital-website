Comprehensive Care for Every Stage of Life At EKA Hospital, we offer a wide range of healthcare services tailored to meet your unique needs. From routine check-ups to advanced treatments, our expert team is dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality care. Whether you're managing a condition or seeking preventive care, we're here to support your health and well-being.

Emergency Services

Immediate medical attention for urgent health issues, including trauma, accidents, heart attacks, and other life-threatening conditions.

Surgery

Surgical interventions for diagnosing, treating, or removing disease or injury. Includes general surgery and specialized areas like orthopedic, cardiovascular, and neurosurgery.

Outpatient Services

Medical care that doesn't require an overnight stay. Includes consultations, diagnostic tests, minor surgeries, and follow-up appointments.

Inpatient Care

Treatment provided to patients who need to stay overnight for observation, treatment, or recovery from surgery or serious illnesses.

Maternity Care

Care for pregnant women, childbirth, and newborns. Includes labor, delivery and postnatal care.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Specialized care for critically ill patients needing constant monitoring and advanced medical support.

Radiology and Imaging Services

Diagnostic imaging such as X-rays, MRI, CT scans, and ultrasounds to diagnose and monitor medical conditions.

Laboratory Services

Medical testing of blood, urine, and other samples to diagnose diseases, monitor treatments, and support medical decisions.

Pharmacy

Dispenses medications prescribed by doctors and provides advice on medication management and side effects.

Rehabilitation Services

Physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy to help patients recover from surgery, injury, or illness.
